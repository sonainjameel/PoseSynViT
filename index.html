Abstract

PoseSynViT is a scalable Vision Transformer designed for Human Pose Estimation (HPE) tasks. This project explores the potential of simple, non-hierarchical ViTs with a lightweight decoder, achieving state-of-the-art results on the MS COCO dataset while maintaining computational efficiency. The proposed model focuses on simplicity, scalability, and transferability, making it suitable for diverse deployment environments.
